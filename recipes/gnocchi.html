In One Pan Gnocchi Roast

A creamy and flavoursome gnocchi bake that only needs one baking dish!

Ingredients

  • 500g Gnocchi
  • 300g Cherry Tomatoes
  • 2 Red Peppers
  • 1 Tablespoon Smoked Paprika
  • 1 Teaspoon Chilli Flakes - or to taste
  • 150g Mascarpone

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 180C.
  2. Chop the peppers into bitesize chunks.
  3. Add the gnocchi, tomatoes and peppers to the baking dish.
  4. Mix in the spices.
  5. Bake for 30 minutes, making sure to mix the contents every 10 minutes.
  6. Remove from oven and mix in the mascarpone
